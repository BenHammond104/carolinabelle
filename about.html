About The Carolina Belle

Classic charm meets reliable service for your special day.

Our Story

The Carolina Belle Trolley Co. brings classic charm to modern weddings and events. We're a local team based in Anderson, South Carolina, and we care about two things: warm hospitality and reliable service.

Our trolley is spotless and ready for photos; our driver is friendly, on time, and prepared. We coordinate with your planner and venue so your day runs smoothly from first stop to grand exit.
