Policies

Retainer & Payment

  • Deposit: $600 due to reserve your date (non-refundable)
  • Balance: Due 14 days before the event

Overtime

Overtime billed at $250/hour in 15-minute increments

Additional Fees

  • Holiday Events: +$150
  • Late-Night Service (ending after 11 PM): +$100

Cancellation Policy

  • Deposits are nonrefundable under any circumstances
  • Cancellations within 30 days of the event date are subject to payment in full
  • If the Company must cancel due to mechanical failure, severe weather, or unforeseen circumstances, all payments (including deposit) will be refunded in full

Damage & Cleaning Policy

  • No alcoholic beverages permitted aboard the trolley
  • $250 cleaning/damage fee for excessive cleanup, damage, or bodily fluids
  • No glitter, confetti, or loose florals
  • Food allowed only with prior approval
  • No smoking inside the trolley
